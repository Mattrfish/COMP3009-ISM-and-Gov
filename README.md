# Information Security Management & Governance – Coursework Repository

## Overview

This repository contains all coursework deliverables for the **COMP3009 – Information Security Management & Governance** module.

The module focuses on understanding the principles and practices of **Information Security Management (ISM)** and **Governance**. It covers organisational security processes, risk analysis, legal compliance, and the non-technical factors contributing to cybersecurity incidents.

---

## Coursework 1 – Literature Analysis & Report Outline

In this assignment, I conducted a detailed analysis of the core literature relevant to the provided scenario (cyber incidents in the healthcare sector).

The analysis involved:  

- Reviewing academic articles, surveys, and relevant reports.  
- Critically analysing non-technical factors that contribute to security incidents.  
- Developing a well-reasoned report outline with Level 1 and Level 2 headings.  
- Providing content descriptors and methodology for literature selection and analysis.  

The Report Outline, can be found in the **CW1** folder.

---

## Coursework 2 – Individual Report

This assignment builds on Coursework 1 and focuses on applying ISM principles to a real-world scenario (**NHS cyber incidents**).

The report involves:  

- Analysing recent healthcare cyber incidents and identifying non-technical reasons for their occurrence.  
- Creating an **asset inventory** with associated **risk analysis**.  
- Drafting a **security policy** outlining roles, responsibilities, and best practices.  
- Designing a **training and awareness plan** for staff.  
- Discussing relevant **UK and EU laws** to ensure legislative compliance.

The final report and supporting materials are located in the **CW2** folder.
